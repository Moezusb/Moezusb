## What I Have Built

### 🔴 [Project Oracle V2: Revenue-Aware Action Engine](https://github.com/Moezusb/meeting-to-jira-pipeline)
`Python` `Pydantic` `Matplotlib` `Pandas`

Python pipeline that reads a raw meeting transcript, extracts every action item, classifies each one by type and priority, and cross-references the output against a Revenue Bridge database to auto-escalate tickets involving at-risk enterprise clients. Pydantic-validated. Zero schema errors. Ships Jira-ready JSON in under a second.

> The part I am most proud of is not the classification engine. It is the Revenue Intelligence Gate -- the idea that a bug blocking a $120k at-risk client is not a "Medium" ticket by any honest measure, and that a pipeline should know the difference.

---

### 📉 [Revenue Bridge Analytics](https://github.com/Moezusb/revenue-bridge-analytics)
`Python` `Pandas` `Matplotlib` `CRM Data`

Three-stage Python pipeline that merges CRM, Support, and Product usage data to surface silent churn risk before it becomes a cancellation conversation. Most churn analyses happen after the warning signs were already there. This one is designed to catch the accounts that look fine on paper and aren't.

### 🎯 [Support Triage Engine](https://github.com/Moezusb/support-triage-engine)
`Python` `Classification` `NLP` `Operations`

Rule-based classification pipeline processing 1,000 enterprise support tickets across 7 categories -- PR Risk, Technical Bug, Churn Risk, Sales, Access Issue, Product Question, Praise -- at 87.2% overall accuracy. Built to isolate the tickets that cannot wait from the ones that can.

### ⚖️ [AI Governance Framework](https://github.com/Moezusb/ai-governance-framework)
`Strategic Framework` `Ethics` `Enterprise AI` `Ops Playbook`

A deployment playbook for enterprise AI -- not written from a policy template, but from the experience of actually shipping AI in a B2B SaaS environment. Covers risk classification, behavioral dynamics (automation bias, the bystander effect in incident response), triple-gate safety protocols, and what governance actually unlocks commercially.
